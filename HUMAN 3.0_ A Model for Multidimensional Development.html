The Three Levels of Development

Within each quadrant, there are 3 macro levels of development:

  • Human 1.0 (The Conformist) – Values established authority and traditions
  • Human 2.0 (The Individualist) – Rejects the norm and pursues their own goals
  • Human 3.0 (The Synthesist) – Able to adopt multiple perspectives and connect patterns

Human 1.0: The Conformist

Characteristics:

  • Values established authority and traditions
  • Narrow-mindedness and black and white thinking
  • Believes there is only "one right way"
  • Often stems from childhood conditioning
  • Finds comfort in following external rules

"Level 1 is similar to an NPC or non-player character running on a script"

Development at this level focuses on acquiring basic knowledge and skills within a domain, but lacks the perspective to see beyond established frameworks.

Human 2.0: The Individualist

Characteristics:

  • Rejects the norm and pursues their own goals
  • Desires to acquire status and be perceived as valuable
  • Less narrow-minded than 1.0
  • Believes that their way is the one right way
  • Focuses on personal achievement and differentiation

"Level 2 is the main character choosing their storyline"

At this level, individuals develop their own identity and values, often in opposition to what they were taught, but may still lack the ability to integrate multiple perspectives.

Human 3.0: The Synthesist

Characteristics:

  • Able to adopt multiple perspectives
  • Connects various patterns of reality
  • Strategizes new paths
  • Understands that all perspectives hold truths
  • Creates holistic and mutually beneficial solutions

"Level 3 is the programmer who can create new games that others also enjoy playing"

At this level, individuals can synthesize knowledge across domains and create new frameworks that transcend traditional boundaries.

The Three Phases of Development

1. Dissonance

Tired of current stage, unsure what comes next

2. Uncertainty

Step into unknown, open to new knowledge

3. Discovery

Find tools and insights for next level

Traits and Channels

Traits

Horizontal development within each phase

  • Knowledge acquisition
  • Skill development
  • Experience gathering

"One can be knowledgeable in fitness, but without practice and experience, they become the fat personal trainer archetype."

Channels

Rapid progress through focused immersion

  • Exciting quest in a video game
  • Rabbit hole of knowledge or skill
  • State of flow where time passes quickly

Examples: Deep meditation, new diet obsession, mystical experiences, career clarity

Metatypes

Synthesis of a person's four archetypes
